Government Schemes

  • Soil Health Card Scheme
    The Soil Health Card Scheme is an initiative by the Government of India aimed at promoting sustainable agriculture by providing farmers with soil health cards. These cards contain information about the health of their soil and recommendations for suitable fertilizers and other agricultural inputs based on soil testing.
    View Site
  • National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture
    The National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) is a comprehensive initiative launched to address issues related to sustainable agriculture in India. It focuses on promoting climate-resilient and environmentally sustainable agricultural practices to enhance productivity and improve farmers' livelihoods.
    View Site
  • Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
    The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a crop insurance scheme launched by the Government of India to provide financial support to farmers in the event of crop loss or damage due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases. It aims to reduce the financial risks faced by farmers and encourage them to adopt modern agricultural practices.
    View Site
  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi
    Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a central sector scheme that provides income support to small and marginal farmers across India. Under this scheme, eligible farmers receive direct income support of Rs. 6,000 per year, paid in three equal installments, to help them meet their agricultural expenses and improve their financial stability.
    View Site
  • Tamil Nadu Agricultural University
    Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU) is a leading agricultural university in India located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. It offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in agriculture, horticulture, agricultural engineering, and other related disciplines. TNAU also conducts research and extension activities to promote agricultural development in the region.
    View Site
  • National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD)
    NABARD is a development financial institution in India that focuses on providing credit and other financial services for agriculture and rural development. It plays a crucial role in financing and promoting agricultural and rural development projects, including agricultural infrastructure, rural industries, and microfinance initiatives.
    View Site
  • National Horticulture Board
    The National Horticulture Board (NHB) is an autonomous organization under the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India. It works towards promoting the production and marketing of horticultural crops such as fruits, vegetables, flowers, and medicinal plants. NHB provides financial assistance, technical guidance, and market support to horticulture growers and entrepreneurs to enhance horticultural productivity and profitability.
